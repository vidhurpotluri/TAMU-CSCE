ABOUT ME

I am a junior-year student at Texas A&M University, College Station currently pursuing a computer science major and an economics minor. I have experience working with cloud infrastructure services and databases for several different companies in various industries.

I was born in Wayne, Michigan on August 20th 1999. I left the States in 2005 to live in Hyderabad, India with my parents and extended family. I lived there until I was accepted into Texas A&M University in the fall of 2017. I started my formal education at A&M wanting to be a mechanical engineer. During my first year at Texas A&M, I met with computer science professors and professionals which motivated me to explore the possibility of a degree in computer science. I took two computer science and engineering classes and discovered that programming and logic are my strengths. I travelled to Seattle and spent a lot of my time there speaking to Amazon and Microsoft employees. I saw firsthand the broad variety of exciting careers the field has to offer and decided to join the promising world of computer science.
